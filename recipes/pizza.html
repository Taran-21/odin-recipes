Pizza Recipe

Pizza Description

Pizza is either round or square - doughy food covered in cheese, tomato sawce and other toppings

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sawce
  • Cheese
  • Secret Blend of Herbs and Spices
  • Variety of toppings

How to Make

  1. Kneed dough and then shape in circle
  2. Add sawce to dough and spread out
  3. Add cheese and toppings on top of sawce
  4. Place in oven on high heat for 10 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
