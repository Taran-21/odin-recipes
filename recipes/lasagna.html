Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna Description

Here is a picture showing a scrumptous piece of lasagna being lifted

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sawce
  • Cheese
  • Pasta Sheets
  • Various Veggies
  • White Sawce

How to Make Lasagna

  1. Boil pastry sheets
  2. Layer in dish with cheese, tomato sawce and veggies
  3. Place in oven on medium heat
  4. Enjoy!
